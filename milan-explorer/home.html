Discover Milan

Welcome to Milan, a bustling metropolis where fashion, history, and modernity collide. From world-renowned design houses and stunning Gothic architecture to vibrant neighbourhoods and exquisite cuisine, Milan offers an unforgettable experience for every traveler.

Explore iconic landmarks, indulge in aperitivo, and immerse yourself in the energetic Italian lifestyle. This guide will help you uncover the best of Milan.

World Fashion Capital

Explore the Quadrilatero della Moda, home to the world's most prestigious designers.

The Majestic Duomo

Ascend Milan's breathtaking Gothic cathedral for intricate details and panoramic city views.

Artistic Masterpieces

Witness Da Vinci's "The Last Supper," explore art at Brera, or experience opera at La Scala.

Navigli Canals & Aperitivo

Stroll along historic canals and enjoy the vibrant evening atmosphere with Milan's beloved aperitivo.

Sforzesco Castle & History

Explore this imposing fortress housing museums and art collections, including Michelangelo's work.
